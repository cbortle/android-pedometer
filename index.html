Watch your Steps

Ten Thousand Steps is a simple game based on the use of a pedometer on your mobile device. The pedometer runs in the background and awards you points for every step you take. These points can be used in a game that's a cross between an old-school RPG and an interactive narrative. Each steps impacts your in-game character - their stats, their adventures, and their appearance. The game makes walking fun and simple, and encourages you to increase your daily exercise.

Health Targets

This section refers to excerpts from the CDC's Winnable Battles Publications. The app addresses the issues of Nutrition, Physical Activity, and Obesity.

This app aims to address the knowledge, perceptions, and attitudes towards regular exercise as maintained by the general public. Ten Thousand Steps is a simple and rewarding game that provides motivation for consistent, daily exercise, and and pushes the user to achieve age-, weight-, and gender- appropriate goals. It later iterations of the game, the app will also provide timely feedback for the user, to increase motivation and awareness of various health issues.
